CSM AAUP Why join us?

Membership of AAUP is managed through the national organization. Learn more about the benefits of being a member here: https://www.aaup.org/membership which include:

  • Standing up for Academic Freedom
  • Securing faculty participation in Shared Governance
  • Protecting the rights of contingent (adjunct) faculty, post-doctoral fellows, and graduate students
  • Receive Academe (Click Here)
  • Get health insurance
